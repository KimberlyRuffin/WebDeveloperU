Dev Q&A Tea Time
Questions Dev Answers
In Spiritfarer,are the Spirts based on real people? The characters and their stories are inspired by the human experience and relatives of the team members who have died
I love the style and visuals in Cuphead. Is it all hand-drawn? We also love it! And it's a good thing too, because it takes a whole pile of work to bring this 1930's aesthetic to Cuphead in the truest form we possibly can. We are heavily inspired by vintage Fleischer Studios and Disney hand-drawn animation, but still delve into other classic studios for inspiration - ComiColor, Van Beuren, Columbia Pictures, Copley Pictures and more.
What's new in Silksong's gameplay? Movement and combat are noticeably faster. Hornet can vault over enemies, climb edges, and perform mid-air combos. The healing and ability systems have been redesigned to use Silk, a new energy resource.
Will there be a Stardew Valley 2? Currently Stardew Valley is still very relavant and hasn't ended yet there are more updates in the future. ConcernedApe also commented that he is in the process of creating a new game called Haunted Chocolatier.

Spiritfarer

Spiritfarer is a cozy management game about dying. You play Stella, ferrymaster to the deceased, a Spiritfarer. Build a boat to explore the world, then befriend and care for spirits before finally releasing them into the afterlife. Content Rating: T, Language, Suggestive Themes, Violent References, Use of Tobacco. Developers: Thunder Lotus. Platforms:Xbox One, PS4, PC,and Switch. Initial Release: Aug 18, 2020.

Cuphead

Cuphead and Mugman traverse strange worlds, acquire new weapons, learn powerful supermoves and uncover hidden secrets in Cuphead. Content Rating: E ,Fantasy Violence, Mild Language, Use of Alcohol and Tobacco. Developers: StudioMDHRContent. Platforms:Xbox One, PS4, PC,and Switch. Initial Release: Sep 29, 2017.

Hollow Knight: Silksong

Hollow Knight: Silksong is the epic sequel to the award winning Hollow Knight. As the lethal hunter Hornet, journey to all-new lands, discover new powers, battle vast hordes of bugs and beasts and uncover ancient secrets tied to your nature and your past. Content Rating: E ,Fantasy Violence, Mild Blood. Developers: Team Cherry Platforms:Xbox One,Xbox Series X/S, PS4,PS5, PC, Switch and Switch 2. Initial Release: Sep 4, 2025.

Hellblade: Senua's Sacrifice

Hellblade: Senua's Sacrifice tells of a warrior's brutal journey into myth and madness. Set in the Viking age, a broken Celtic warrior embarks on a haunting vision quest into Viking Hell to fight for the soul of her dead lover.Content Rating: M ,Strong Language, Blood and Gore, Intense Violence. Developers: Ninja Theory. Platforms:Xbox One,Xbox Series X/S, PS4, PC,and Switch. Initial Release: Aug 8, 2017.

Hades II

Hades II is the sequel to Supergiant's hit game Hades, mixing engaging story-telling and roguelite action elements for an engaging dungeon-crawling experience. Battle beyond the Underworld to vanquish the Titan of Time as the Princess of the Dead in this bewitching sequel to the original rogue-like dungeon crawler. Hades II lets players explore a bigger, deeper mythic world with the full might of Olympus at their call in a story that reacts to their every setback and accomplishment.Content Rating: T ,Mild Language, Violence, Suggestive Themes, Blood, Alcohol Reference. Developers: Supergiant Games. Platforms: PC, Switch and Switch 2. Initial Release: May 5, 2024.

Stardew Valley

In the open-ended country life RPG Stardew Valley, you've inherited your grandfather's old farm plot. Armed with hand-me-down tools and a few coins, you set out to begin your new life.Content Rating: E ,Mild Language, Fantasy Violence, Simulated Gambling, Mild Blood, Use of Tobacco and/or Alcohol. Developers: ConcernedApe, Sickhead Games. Platforms: Platforms:Xbox One, PS4, PC,Switch and Switch 2. Initial Release: Feb 26, 2016.
